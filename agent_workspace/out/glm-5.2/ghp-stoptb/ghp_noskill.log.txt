PALETTE / MOTIF
- Primary: deep navy #1F3864 (panels, dark slides, chips, table headers, title ink on light slides via Georgia bold).

- Recurring motif: a numbered 6-step "Performance management" process chain (Set objectives → Set targets → Establish clear metrics → Track & disseminate metrics → Review performance → Celebrate & act) appearing on framework-processes and challenges-processes; numbered navy chips recur on the contents agenda and phase timelines; square (▪) level-0 bullets with en-dash (–) sub-bullets deck-wide.
- Light content slides; dark navy cover (navy left sidebar + light right panel) and dark navy closing slide bookend the deck.

STRUCTURE (one slide per ## section in spec.md, in order): cover, contents, background, goals, phases, framework-processes, framework-terms (table), framework-enablers (2x2), challenges-processes, challenges-enablers (2x2), strengths, agreed-issues (table), gdf-kpi-burden (quote-card grid 4+3), gdf-8-steps (2x4 grid), gdf-kpi-tree (table), gdf-dashboard (clustered column chart + side table), gdf-next-steps (action table), advocacy-3-steps, advocacy-filters (4-stage funnel), cmb-objectives-tree, cmb-targets (table), cfcs-mission, mdr-barometer (2x2), next-steps (dark closing).

HELPERS: Reused reference_build_deck.py helpers verbatim (textbox, paragraph, rectangle, connector, style_run, _apply_bullet, title, chart, table, format_chart, source_line, page_number, bullet_mixed, caption_box) plus custom labeled_rect, framed_col/framed_row, step_chip/process_chain, quote_card, step_card, quad_grid.
