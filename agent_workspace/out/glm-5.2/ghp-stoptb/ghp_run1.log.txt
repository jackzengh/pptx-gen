All one blue family + neutrals (<=6 non-neutral hues).
- Repeated motif: (1) short accent connector under every action title (deck spine), (2) numbered 6-step "Performance management" process chain (oval navy chips on an accent line) reused on framework-processes and challenges-processes, (3) navy-headed framed content boxes with steel header bands, (4) full-bleed accent bar on the dark cover/closing bookends.

BUILD APPROACH: Copied the skill's reusable helpers (textbox, paragraph, _apply_bullet, style_run, rectangle, connector, title, chart, table, bullet_mixed) and the reference's source/page-number/footer patterns, then wrote my own framed_box/box_bullets/number_chip/process_chain helpers. Used CANONICAL role prefixes only: background_* decor rectangles for framed-box fields + header bands + cards + chips (overlaid textboxes are overlap-exempt and grid-excluded); true <p:bg> for full-bleed dark/light backgrounds; overlay_caption_* directly above every chart and table; meta_* source lines + page numbers + chrome labels; overlay_eyebrow for the two eyebrow slides; chartpart_* for the filter arrows. Named every shape.

- Build 3: sandbox check_pptx ok=true (with and without Pillow); re-ran finish_deck.
